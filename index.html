Batch Image Processor

What does it Do?

Import hundreds of photos and perform various manipulations on them!

  • Rotate
    • Angular
    • Auto-Portrait
    • Auto-Landscape
  • Resize
    • Smart resizing, matches the destination size to the aspect ratio of your images
  • Crop
  • Watermarks
    • Text Watermark
    • Image Watermarks
    • Variable Opacity
  • Adjustments
    • Brightness
    • Contrast
    • Gamma
    • Saturation, or
      • Greyscale / Sepia
  • Rename

And more!
